- **Element selection mode** lets you pick and save specific elements on a page instead of capturing the whole thing, giving you finer control over what goes into MindPocket.
- **Extension device login** adds a sign-in flow right inside the extension popup, so you can authenticate without switching tabs or copying tokens.
- **Bookmark view mode selection** lets you choose how bookmarks appear, making it easier to browse large collections the way you prefer.
- **Simplified login** by removing two-factor authentication, reducing friction during sign-in.
